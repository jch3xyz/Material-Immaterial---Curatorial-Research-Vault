# Material / Immaterial

A curatorial research vault — a cross-referenced web of **concepts, arguments, tensions, symbols, metaphors, and reading pathways** drawn from sixteen books by thirteen authors, tracing the seam between the material and the immaterial across art, media, systems, embodiment, and surveillance.

Every note is part of a single interlinked graph. Follow the links, or start from one of the indexes below.

- [[Home|The dashboard]] — the main navigation entry point

- [[Argument Index]] — claims and theses, by author and by theme
- [[Tension Index]] — the oppositions and paradoxes
- [[Author Network]] — the thirteen authors and how they connect
- [[Reading Pathways]] — curated nonlinear paths through the corpus

## What this is

An interpretive layer — a reading, not a reproduction. Notes compress and connect ideas from their sources, with each claim carrying a confidence label and a citation back to the passage that grounds it.
