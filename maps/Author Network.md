The Beyond Modern Sculpture ingest adds [[Jack Burnham]] as a new author node — the first art/systems-esthetics primary source after Lippard — and realizes the long-standing Lippard ↔ Burnham forward-stub along with four references: Burnham ↔ Lippard (the keystone dematerialization / object-to-system bridge, both 1968-era), Burnham ↔ Haraway (Cyborg Art 1968 anticipating the 1985 cyborg), Burnham ↔ Hayles (cybernetics, systems, and nervous-system models as a precursor to the posthuman body), and Burnham ↔ McLuhan (1960s technology-and-culture contemporaries; the electric/cybernetic age and the light bulb as pure information).)
