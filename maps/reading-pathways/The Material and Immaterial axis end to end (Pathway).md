# The Material and Immaterial axis end to end

The vault's titular axis runs from the solidity of the made object to its dissolution into information, fiction, data, and behavioral surplus. This master pathway traces that hierarchy end to end through the ten-author spine, watching each source in turn contest, affirm, invert, hold, date, or dissolve the boundary between the material and the immaterial. It consolidates every per-book "Material/Immaterial across the vault" path into a single curatorial through-line, useful for staging the vault's central tension as one continuous argument — from McLuhan's electric reversal and Burnham's object-to-system migration, through Lippard's dematerialized art object and Hayles's disembodied information, to Haraway's coded control grid, Harari's intersubjective fictions, Kurzweil's affirmative transcendence, and Zuboff's rendition of lived experience into extractable data.
