# About This Extraction

Cleanup performed: HTML-to-text extraction, Unicode NFKC normalization, removal of page labels/source-document boilerplate/running page markers, light OCR cleanup, section splitting, note separation, conservative readability wrapping, and conservative replacement of unreadable symbol-like runs with a marker.
